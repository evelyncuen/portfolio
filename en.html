Evelyn Cuen

Digital Designer

Inmersive experience "Viaje al Mictlán"

Participation in the design and animation of an immersive experience narrating the journey through the 7 levels of Mictlán (the Mexica underworld), projected for the Day of the Dead celebration at Centro Cultural de las Fronteras, UACJ.

Inmersive experience "Do de pecho"

Participation in the planning, design, and animation of an immersive experience created as a tribute to the Mexican artist Juan Gabriel, representing his origins, the Bellas Artes concert, his achievements, and his influence on other artists. Projected at Centro Cultural de las Fronteras, UACJ.

"The infinity"

Animated short film for the final project in the 2D Animation class. In sync with the song "Infinity" by Yuuri, it shows a girl meeting a dog and deciding to make it her lifelong companion.

The Fox vs. The Ball

Animated short film created for the 3D Animation class. It plays with movements and camera angles to depict a comedic conflict between a little fox and a ball that accidentally falls on him.

Motion Graphics

Throughout my career, I have acquired skills in animating motion graphics, including texts, images, shapes, and special effects, used in various contexts such as projections, videos, among others.

Platform: PC

Serious game to raise awareness about accessibility measures for people with color blindness in video games. The story follows Eloise, a game developer, and her pet, Kiwi, who guides her through different scenarios while they learn interactively.
